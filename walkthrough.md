## Demonstrated Features

### 1. Modern Typography & Ambient Accents
* **Serif Headings & Sans-Serif Details**: Curated Google Fonts combination (`Cormorant Garamond` and `Playfair Display`) create a premium, high-end visual tone.
* **Warm HSL Coffee Tones**: Background elements and shadows use deep dark roast colors (`#0d0500`) accented by warm caramel and gold hues.

### 2. Micro-Animations & Intersections
* **Cinematic Scroll Entries**: Scroll-triggered entry classes (`.fade-up`, `.fade-left`, `.fade-right`) fade and glide elements in dynamically as the viewport scrolls.
* **Interactive Coffee Selection Tabs**: Category tabs for beverages adapt and reveal drink selections seamlessly.

### 3. Interactive Reservation Form
* Includes full guest selection, dynamic submission tracking with a loading filler effect, and a success confirmation state once secured.

### Final Setup Step
To activate your live link, please enable GitHub Actions deployment on your repository settings:
1. Open [https://github.com/priyanshibuild/brewcraft/settings/pages](https://github.com/priyanshibuild/brewcraft/settings/pages) in your browser.
2. Under **Build and deployment** > **Source**, change the dropdown from **Deploy from a branch** to **GitHub Actions**.
3. Once enabled, the deployment workflow will trigger automatically and build your site. You can watch the progress in the **Actions** tab.
